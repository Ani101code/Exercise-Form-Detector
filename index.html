üéØ Project Aim

The aim of this project is to develop an AI-powered exercise form analysis system that uses computer vision to analyze human movement in real time, provide feedback on exercise technique, and count exercise repetitions automatically.

The system detects body posture from live video input and evaluates joint positions and movement patterns to identify incorrect form, asymmetries, and unsafe movements. It tracks movement cycles to accurately count repetitions (reps), helping users monitor training volume while reducing injury risk and improving exercise efficiency.

üé• Real-time Analysis

Live video input with instant form feedback using MediaPipe pose estimation (33 landmarks). 10-frame median smoothing for accuracy.

üìä Angle Calculation

Precise joint angle measurement (elbow, hip, knee, back) with vector-based calculation and hysteresis filtering.

üîÑ Smart Rep Counting

Automatic repetition tracking with form validation, 2-second cooldown, and state machine for each exercise.

‚ö†Ô∏è Form Correction

Identifies incorrect form: sagging hips, kipping, knee valgus, asymmetry, and partial range of motion.

üîä Audio Feedback

Verbal cues for form correction using TTS (pyttsx3/win32com) with intelligent cooldown system.

üìà Session Tracking

Track reps across push-ups, pull-ups, and squats with individual reset (R) and session reset (T) options.

üìã Strict Form Requirements

Based on biomechanics research and exercise science literature. Reps only count when ALL criteria are met.

üí™ PUSH-UP

  • Down position: Elbow < 90 deg
  • Up position: Elbow > 160 deg
  • Body angle: 160-180 deg (straight)
  • No sagging or piking
  • No knee pushups (knee > 150 deg)
  • Stand SIDEWAYS to camera

‚¨ÜÔ∏è PULL-UP

  • Top position: Elbow < 80 deg
  • Bottom position: Elbow > 150 deg
  • Body angle: > 160 deg (no kipping)
  • Keep legs still
  • Pull evenly with both arms
  • Full range of motion required

ü¶µ SQUAT

  • Up position: Knee > 150 deg (standing)
  • Down position: Knee < 110 deg (parallel+)
  • Chest up: Back angle 60-120 deg
  • Knees track over toes
  • No knee valgus (caving in)
  • Face camera directly
